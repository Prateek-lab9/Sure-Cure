Our Motto

Our website is dedicated to making organ donation a seamless process for everyone. We provide a simple and digital way to create a pledge form for organ donation. By filling out our user-friendly form, you can officially declare your wish to contribute to saving lives.

After completing the pledge, users are encouraged to submit their form to NGOs or the organ transplant department of hospitals, along with a family witness to make it legally binding.

In addition, we support users by generating an official PDF pledge certificate that can be downloaded and submitted offline for registration. Our goal is to increase awareness and encourage more individuals to become organ donors, helping save countless lives.

NOTTO

The National Organ and Tissue Transplant Organization (NOTTO) is a government agency under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, dedicated to promoting organ donation across India. It serves as the central coordinating body for organ transplantation, ensuring the safe and ethical distribution of organs to those in need. NOTTO's mission is to increase awareness about organ donation, maintain a transparent registry, and facilitate the smooth exchange of organs across the country.

Organ Donation Pledge Form

I, the undersigned, hereby pledge to donate my organs and/or tissues after my death to help save lives and improve health for others. I make this decision voluntarily and with full understanding of its importance.

Organs/Tissues to Donate:

Important Instructions:

1.Witness Requirement: You must bring a family member as a witness who will sign this form alongside you. The witness will confirm that this pledge is voluntary and understood by you.

2.Submit this pledge form at an authorized location: To make this pledge official, submit your signed form to any of the following:

  • Organ Donation Registration Camps
  • NGOs working in the field of organ donation (e.g., MOHAN Foundation)
  • The Organ & Transplant Department at a hospital

3.Required Documents: Along with this form, bring your valid ID proof (e.g., Aadhaar, Voter ID) for verification.

This form is not legally binding unless submitted to the relevant authorities. The official registration with NOTTO or your state organ donation registry must be done at one of the authorized locations mentioned above.
