Workout Plans

Beginner

  • 10 min walk + stretching
  • 15 bodyweight squats
  • 10 pushups (knee-modified)
  • 20 jumping jacks

Intermediate

  • 30 min jog or cycling
  • 3 sets of 15 squats
  • 3 sets of 10 pushups
  • Plank – 30 sec hold x 3

Nutrition Tips

  • Eat whole foods: fruits, vegetables, lean protein
  • Drink 2–3 liters of water daily
  • Track calories using MyFitnessPal
  • Plan 3 meals + 2 healthy snacks a day
  • Avoid processed sugar and white flour
